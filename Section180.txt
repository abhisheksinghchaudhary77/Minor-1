Section No. 180
Name: Permiting other to Drive
Clauses:
	(1) If person fails to satisy provisions of Section 3 and 4
	Sub-Clauses:
		(a) No License: If a person permits other person who either does not have license or had not attained the age of majority
		Punishment: 3 months imprisonment or Rs.1000 Fine or both

		(b) Not of the age of majority: If a person permits other person who either does not have license or had not attained the age of majority
		Punishment: 3 months imprisonment or Rs.1000 Fine or both
