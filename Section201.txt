Section No. 201
Name: Keeping disabled vehicle for causing impediment to traffic
Clauses:
	(1) Obstruction to Traffic: Whoever keeps  a disabled  vehicle on  any public  place,  in  such a manner, so  as to  cause impediment to the free flow of 	    traffic, shall be liable  for penalty  up to  fifty rupees  per hour,  so long  as it remains in that position.
	    Punishment: Rs.50 per hour
