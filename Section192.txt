Section No. 192
Name: Using vehicle which is not Registered  or vehichel without route permit
Clauses:
	(1) Regular Purpose
	Sub-Clauses:
		(a) If  the vehicle used in the commission of the offence is a motor car, suspend its certificate of registration for a period not exceeding four 		    months: Nothing in this section shall apply to the use of a motor vehicle in an emergency for the conveyance of persons suffering from sickness or 		    injury or for the transport of materials for repair or for the transport of food or materials to relieve distress or of medical supplies for a like 		    purpose other vehicles which are not registered shall be punished.
		Punishment: 

		(b) First Offence: Nothing in this section shall apply to the use of a motor vehicle in an emergency for the conveyance of persons suffering from 		            sickness or injury or for the transport of materials for repair or for the transport of food or materials to relieve distress or of medical 		            supplies for a like purpose other vehicles which are not registered shall be punished.
		Punishment: Rs.5000 Fine

		(c) Second or subsequent offence: Nothing in this section shall apply to the use of a motor vehicle in an emergency for the conveyance of persons 		    	    suffering from sickness or injury or for the transport of materials for repair or for the transport of food or materials to relieve distress or of 		    	    medical supplies for a like purpose other vehicles which are not registered shall be punished.
		Punishment: 1 year imprisonment or Rs.1000 Fine or both

	(2) Medical Emergency
	Sub-Clauses:
		(a) If  the vehicle used in the commission of the offence is a  transport  vehicle,  suspend  its  permit  for  a  period  not exceeding six months or 		    	    cancel it: Nothing in this section shall apply to the use of a motor vehicle in an emergency for the conveyance of persons suffering from sickness 		    	    or injury or for the transport of materials for repair or for the transport of food or materials to relieve distress or of medical supplies for a 		    	    like purpose other vehicles which are not registered shall be punished.
		Punishment: No punishment
