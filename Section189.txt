Section No. 189
Name: Racing or Trial in public place
Clauses:
	(1) Racing in public place without state's concent
	Sub-Clauses:
		(a) Racing in public place without state's concent or trial of speed: Whoever without  the  written consent of  the State  Government permits  or 		    takes  part in a race or trial of  speed of any kind between motor vehicles in any public place shall be  punishable
		Punishment: 1 month imprisonment and Rs.500 Fine
