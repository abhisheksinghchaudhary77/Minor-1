Section No. 185
Name: Drink and Drive or under the influence of Drugs
Clauses:
	(1) Blood contains Drugs
	Sub-Clauses:
		(a) First Offence: If a person driving a vehicle is driving by drinking or under the influence of Drugs
		Punishment: 6 months imprisonment and Rs.2000 Fine

		(b) Second or subsequent offence: If a person driving a vehicle is driving by drinking or under the influence of Drugs
		Punishment: 2 years imprisonment and Rs.3000 Fine
