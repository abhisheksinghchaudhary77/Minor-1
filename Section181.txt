Section No. 181
Name: Contravening the provisions of Driving Licence
Clauses:
	(1) If person fails to satisy provisions of Section 3 and 4
	Sub-Clauses:
		(a) No License: If he is having invalid lisence or did not attained the age of majority
		Punishment: 3 months imprisonment or Rs.500 Fine or both

		(b) Not of the age of majority: If he is having invalid lisence or did not attained the age of majority
		Punishment: 3 months imprisonment or Rs.500 Fine or both
