Section No. 194
Name: Overloading of Vehichle
Clauses:
	(1) Any  driver of  a vehicle  who refuses to stop and submit his vehicle to  weighing after  being directed  to do  so  by  an  officer
	Sub-Clauses:
		(a) Driving or causing to drive exceeding permissible weight:  Any  driver of  a vehicle  who refuses to stop and submit his vehicle to  weighing 	     	    after  being directed  to do  so  by  an  officer authorised in  this if the officer found it overloaded.
		Punishment: Rs.2000 and Rs.1000 per ton along with overloading Rs.3000
