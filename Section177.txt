Section No. 177
Name: Contravention of Rules and Regulations
Clauses:
	(1) If no penelty is provided
	Sub-Clauses:
		(a) First Offence: Contravention of Provisions, Rules Regulations and Notifications
		Punishment: Rs.100 Fine

		(b) Second or subsequent Offence: Contravention of Provisions, Rules Regulations and Notifications 
		Punishment: Rs.100 Fine
