Section No. 187
Name: Accident Related Offences
Clauses:
	(1) Driver is not performing his duty
	Sub-Clauses:
		(a) For offence related to accident: For offences relating to accident, when driver is not able to perform his duty when any accident occured such as 		    Hit and Run
		    Punishment: 3-6 months imprisonment or Rs.500 to Rs.1000 Fine or both
