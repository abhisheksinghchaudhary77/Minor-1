Section No. 186
Name: Driving while Suffering from diseases
Clauses:
	(1) Driving while Suffering From any Dieseases that could harm his concious
	Sub-Clauses:
		(a) First Offence: Whoever drives a motor vehicle in any public place when he is to his knowledge suffering from  any disease  or disability  		    calculated  to  cause  his driving of  the vehicle  to be a source of danger to the public.
		Punishment: Rs.2000 Fine

		(b) Second or subsequent offence: Whoever drives a motor vehicle in any public place when he is to his knowledge suffering from  any disease  or 		    disability  calculated  to  cause  his driving of  the vehicle  to be a source of danger to the public.
		Punishment: Rs.500 Fine
