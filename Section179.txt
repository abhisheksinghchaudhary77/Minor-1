Section No. 179
Name: Disobedience and Refusal to provide information
Clauses:
	(1) If no penelty is provided
	Sub-Clauses:
		(a) Willful disobedience: If any of the passenger or Citizen refuses to provide information and Disobeys the Authority
		Punishment: 1 month imprisonment or Rs.500 Fine or both

		(b) Refusal to provide inforamtion: If any of the passenger or Citizen refuses to provide information and Disobeys the Authority
		Punishment: 1 month imprisonment or Rs.500 Fine or both
