Section No. 195
Name: Repeating an offence again and again within 3 years
Clauses:
	(1) Repetion of an Offence: Whoever having  been convicted  of an  offence under  this Act  or the rules made  thereunder commits  a  similar  offence  on  a  	    second  or subsequent occasion  within three  years  of  the  commission  of  the previous offence, no court shall, except for reasons to be recorded by it 	    in  writing.
	    Punishment: More than 1/4 of the prescribed Fine
