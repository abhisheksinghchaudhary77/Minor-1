Section No. 188
Name: Unfit for Driving or  Dangerously
Clauses:
	(1) For abbet of an offence
	Sub-Clauses:
		(a) Abetting an offence: Whoever abets the commission of an offence under section 184, section 185 or section 186 shall be punishable with the 		    punishment provided for the offence.
		Punishment: 6 months imprisonment
