Section No. 182
Name: Disqualification
Clauses:
	(1) Disqualification of conductor
	Sub-Clauses:
		(a) Disqualified conductor acting as conductor: Acting as a conductor or obtaining licence by hiding fact which could prevent him from obtaining 		    licence
		Punishment: 1 month imprisonment or Rs.100 Fine and Revocation of licence

	(2) Disqualification of Driver
	Sub-Clauses:
		(a) Hiding any fact related to his age or any other fact which can prevent him from getting licence: Acting as a conductor or obtaining licence by 		    	    hiding fact which could prevent him from obtaining licence
		Punishment: 3 month imprisonment or Rs.500 Fine and Revocation of licence
